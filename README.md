# ResoMUN

**ResoMUN** is a free online platform for managing Model United Nations
(MUN) conferences, including resolution writing, amendment handling,
committee management, and debate tools.

# Getting Started

## Create an Account

1.  Navigate to the ResoMUN homepage.
2.  Click **Create Account**.
3.  Fill in the registration form.
4.  Click **Back to Index**.
5.  Click **Login**.
6.  Enter your email and password.

You are now on your ResoMUN homepage.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Creating and Managing a Conference

## Creating a Conference

1.  Click **Create a new conference**.
2.  Enter a conference title.
3.  Click **Create Conference**.

⚠️ **Important:** - Do NOT leave the conference title empty. - Only
create a conference if you intend to use it.

The creator of the conference automatically becomes the **Secretary
General (SecGen)**.

If everything appears in green, your conference is live.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Managing Your Conference

Go to:

**My ResoMUN → Select Conference → Manage Conference**

From here, you can:

-   Add conference officers
-   Add committees
-   Assign chairs
-   View published resolutions

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Adding Conference Officers

1.  Select a user.
2.  Select a role.
3.  Click **Add Officer**.
4.  Confirm the warning popup.

You can assign multiple officers per role.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Managing Committees

## Creating a Committee

1.  Enter the committee name.
2.  Click **Add Committee**.

Once created, you can:

-   Assign chairs
-   Manage delegates
-   View committee resolutions
-   View submitted amendments

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Assigning Chairs

1.  Click **Change Chairs**.
2.  Select a user.
3.  Click **Add Chair**.

Chairs can later be removed if necessary.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Chair Guide

To manage a committee:

1.  Select the committee.
2.  Click **Manage Committee**.

Chairs can:

-   View all delegates
-   Manually assign delegates to countries
-   Remove delegate assignments
-   View resolutions
-   Enable/disable editing (when debate starts)
-   View and manage amendments

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Delegate Guide

## Register for a Conference

1.  Select a **Conference/Committee**.
2.  Select your **Delegation (Country)**.
3.  Click **Register for Delegation**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Writing a Resolution

1.  Select your conference/committee.
2.  Enter a title.
3.  Click **Create Resolution**.

⚠️ Do NOT leave the title empty.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Using the Editor

You can add:

-   Preambulatory clauses
-   Operative clauses
-   Subclauses
-   Sub-subclauses

Features:

-   Auto-save after every clause
-   Manual save option
-   Automatic punctuation handling
-   Recognized operative and preambulatory words
-   Preview mode
-   Edit mode

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Signing a Resolution

You can sign a resolution if:

-   You are in the correct committee
-   You have not already signed it

Click **Sign Resolution**, draw your signature, and confirm.

If you are the main submitter: - Your signature replaces the
placeholder.

If you are a signatory: - Your name appears alphabetically in the
signatories list.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Amendments

To amend a resolution:

1.  Select:
    -   Add
    -   Edit
    -   Delete
2.  Choose the clause number.
3.  Submit your amendment.

You will receive:

-   A summary
-   A generated PDF for debate

If passed by vote, the amendment updates the resolution automatically.
